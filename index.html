Control CNC machines and robots with open-source software.

Flexible

Create systems to control nearly anything. Machine tools. Laser cutters. 3D printers. Even robots and quadcopters!

Universal

Use Beaglebone Black, Raspberry Pi, ARM devices, or the spare PC in your closet.

Reliable

Configure realtime threads on recent Linux kernels with Xenomai, RTAI, or RT_Preempt threads, and also in non-real time with vanilla Posix threads for simulation and testing.

Get up and running in seconds.
